Hamdard Public School has been founded with the noble aim of providing the best possible education and training facilities that are acceptable to both Madinat al-Hikmah (city of education, science and culture) and the Ministry of Education. While doing this, not only the needs of Pakistani students but also the demands of foreign educational systems have been kept in mind.
